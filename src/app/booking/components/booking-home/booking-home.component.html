What are Instance Reservations?

Instance reservations allow you to foresee your data analysis needs in advance and request the required resources ahead of time.
You can reserve multiple instances with different resources (CPU, RAM, GPU, etc) for a given period of time and VISA will ensure that these are available when you need them.
The instances that you reserve are created using tokens that will be assigned to you once your reservation is accepted. Tokens are valid for the dates of your reservation.
You are free to distribute the tokens to your team or other people you are working with so that they can take advantage of the resources during the reservation period.
